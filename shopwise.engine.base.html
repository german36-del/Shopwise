compute_optimal_supermarket()

Computes the optimal supermarket based on the prices of products in the shopping list.

The method processes the shopping list to categorize products into weight, unit, and liquid types. It then calculates the total prices for each supermarket, optionally printing and saving the scraped data. The optimal supermarket is determined as the one with the lowest total price for the available products.

If valid supermarkets are found, logs the optimal supermarket; otherwise, logs a warning indicating no valid supermarkets were found.

Returns:

None
